Glorious History of Mt. Manaslu Secondary School

A school is a place where people go to learn about topics such as reading, writing and mathematics. In a school or university, one or more teachers help students to learn. Mt. Manaslu has always been a great place for students and teachers to share their knowledge grow their mind.

Established in the year 1986 A.D, Mt. Manaslu has celebrated 34 excellent academic years. The school started with about .... students and now Mt. Manaslu Secondary School is shaping the mind of more than 1200 students. The academic results of this school has always been appreciable. Now the former students of Mt.
