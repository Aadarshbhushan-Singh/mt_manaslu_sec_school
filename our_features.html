OUR FEATURES

Great schools have many distinguishing features in common.This post is not intended to be an exhaustive report on all the characteristics of great schools. It lists, however, some features that most educational experts and practitioners consider to be necessary in the anatomy of any great school.

Karate

We teach our students Shi-To-Ryu Karate which is affiliated by Government Of Nepal. Our student have also won various medals and tournaments.

Computer Lab

We have well faciliated computer lab where students are able to connect to the modern technology and grow their potential.

Library

"Nothing is pleasanter than exploring a library." Our Library has variety of books to enhance and support the learning of the students and teachers.

Music and Dance

Students in our school can also learn Dancing and Music. We believe in overall development of the students.

Variety of Subjects

Our Students can choose variety of optional subjects in class 9 and 10 according to their wish and potential. We have qualified teacher for all subjects.

Other Sports

Except for the karate students in our school can learn different sports like volleyball, tennis, javelin throw, athlatics. We have sports teacher to guide our students.

Prominent Academic Session

Apart from the regular classes we also give extra classes to the students who are weak in particular subject. One to one interaction is done with such students.

Qualified Teachers

Our well educated and qualified teachers always take care that our students receive better education and get full opportunity to develop themselves.
